# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Retail self-traders who are also active learners. Primary job: review real trades to understand what actually happened, not what they thought happened. Secondary job: build and refine a personal playbook, apply discipline rules consistently, and track improvement over time.

Context: used at a desk, in review sessions (post-market or weekend), occasionally during live sessions to log a trade. Not a real-time execution tool. A reflection and accountability tool.

## Product Purpose

A private trading journal for Spanish-speaking traders. Log trades, measure performance against personal rules, review setups, and close the loop between learning resources (playbook, backtests, notes) and real-money decisions.

Success looks like: the user catches their own behavioral patterns before they become costly habits. The journal makes growth measurable, not just felt.

## Brand Personality

Clear · Honest · Growth-oriented

Tone: direct but not harsh. Like a coach reviewing tape — no flattery, no alarm. Names the problem, shows the data, points to next steps. Never cheerful or hype-driven. Never cold or clinical.

## Anti-references

- **Crypto exchange UI** — no dark neon, no glowing gradients, no rocket emojis, no hype energy (Binance / Bybit aesthetic)
- **SaaS dashboard clichés** — no identical card grids, no big hero metrics, no purple-to-blue gradients, no glassmorphism
- **Legacy brokerage platform** — no gray toolbar rows, no unbroken dense tables, no visual entropy from 2008
- **Productivity app** — no Notion / Linear energy: no pastel accents, no emoji-heavy copy, no casual tone, not a to-do list

## Design Principles

1. **Evidence over assertion** — every metric must trace back to actual trade data. The UI shows what happened, not what the user thinks happened. Ambiguous data is shown as ambiguous.
2. **Friction is information** — if logging a trade or review feels uncomfortable, that discomfort is data. The interface doesn't smooth over it. Missing fields and skipped reviews are visible, not hidden.
3. **Honesty at every scale** — losses are not softened. Wins are not amplified beyond their statistical significance. Labels, colors, and copy reflect actual outcomes, not optimistic framings.
4. **No engagement theater** — no streaks, badges, or gamification. The reward is measurable improvement. Nothing in the product exists to drive engagement; everything exists to drive accuracy.
5. **The loop must close** — every trade feeds the playbook and discipline rules; every rule is tested against the trade log. The product actively connects learning to behavior to results.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target: WCAG 2.1 AAA.
- All text: ≥7:1 contrast on backgrounds
- Large text (≥18px regular / ≥14px bold): ≥4.5:1 contrast
- Full keyboard navigation with visible focus rings
- Screen reader support via semantic HTML and ARIA labels
- Reduced-motion alternatives for every animation
- Color never the sole signal for outcome states (win/loss/be): always paired with text or icon
